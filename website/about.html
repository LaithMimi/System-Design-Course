Our story

Here our vision & mission

We wouldn't find anything on the market that made hiring feel human and simple at the same time — so we decided to build our own recruiting software.

Since 2023
01 — The vision

We had the vision to make recruiting and hiring as easy as managing any other business

No clunky enterprise portals. No week-long setups. Just a tool that lets any team publish a great job ad, reach real candidates, and decide together — the same day.

  • ✓ Hiring workflows anyone can run, not just HR
  • ✓ Candidate experience treated as a first-class feature
  • ✓ Free core product, forever
Why choose us

Why choose us?

Three principles guide everything we ship — pick one to see how it shows up in the product.

We ship what actually moves hiring forward

Every quarter we sit with recruiting teams, watch where they lose time, and build for exactly that. AI-assisted screening, one-click multiposting, and collaborative scorecards all started as customer pain, not roadmap filler.

Your process, not ours

Pipelines, stages, scorecards, and templates are fully yours to shape. Saasmo adapts to a two-person startup or a two-hundred-person hiring machine without a consultant in sight.

Better together

Deep integrations with the tools your team already lives in — Slack, LinkedIn, Google Workspace, and 250+ job boards — so hiring happens where the work happens.
